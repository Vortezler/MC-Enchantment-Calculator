How to use it
How it works: the Nether is 8× compressed horizontally, so 1 block walked in the Nether = 8 blocks in the Overworld. Divide your Overworld X and Z by 8 to get the Nether spot to build a portal; multiply Nether X/Z by 8 to see where an existing Nether portal lands you. Y (height) is identical in both dimensions. Coordinates are the same in every Minecraft version.
