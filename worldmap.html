How this works Minecraft's real world generation is enormously complex, so rather than fake it, this page hands your seed to Chunkbase — the trusted tool that computes accurate biomes and structure locations for every version. Pick your edition and version, drop in your coordinates, and hit Locate on anything you're hunting for: it opens the map centered on you, with the nearest one in view. Strongholds and slime chunks open their dedicated finders, which list exact coordinates.
Maps & world data by chunkbase.com. This page just pre-loads your seed and jumps you to the right view. Not affiliated with Chunkbase or Mojang.
Tip: if the version looks off when the map opens, switch it with Chunkbase's own dropdown — your seed stays loaded. Text seeds are hashed exactly like Minecraft does, so they work too.
